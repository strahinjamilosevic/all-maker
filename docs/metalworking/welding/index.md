Around 2016–2017 I decided to get off my ass and create a workshop. An office job did not sit well with my itchy hands and mind so I asked my mom to use one of the unfinished parts of her house.

I ended up clearing a bunch of old, forgotten storage items and little by little built a little place where I could grind, solder, weld, work with wood, and so on. Now, whether it was a good idea to do all of that in the same room—definitely not. But, live and learn.

Here are some of the projects I managed to dig up from my old photos 10 years later. I hope to document some of my learning and create a baseline for the future projects I plan to embark on.

My idea is to share the outcome, demo the process, and pass on the learnings from my mistakes along the way.
